Serves: 4
Cook Time: 30 min

Chef's Salad

This was the gateway salad for me...I realised you could make a salad a meal, and put in it whatever you want. I no longer remember where I learnt it from.

Ingredients

  • 2 cos lettuce, torn into bits
  • 300g box tomatoes, diced and salted
  • pack of ham, diced
  • gruyere cheese, diced
  • Dressing
  • 6 tbsp sunflower oil
  • 1 tbsp white wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp dijon mustard
  • salt and pepper
  • Croutons
  • 4 slices of thick bread, cut into cubes

Method

Make the croutons. Drizzle the bread with olive oil and salt, and cook in the oven on 160Â°C for 10 min. Taste, I aim for the crouton to be crunchy on the outside but still have a bit of give on the inside, they might need another 5 min.

Make the dressing. Whisk together the oil, vinegar, mustard, salt and pepper. If the dressing is a bit thick, thin with 1 tbsp of water.

Assemble the salad. Mix the dressing into the lettuce, then add the tomatoes, ham and cheese and mix round.

Serve onto plates, with the croutons on top.
