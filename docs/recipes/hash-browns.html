Hash Browns

Serves: 4
Cook Time: 1 hour

From BBC Food. Makes 8.

Ingredients

  • 4 floury potatoes (Maris Piper or King Edwards)
  • 1 onion
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • salt and pepper
  • sunflower oil

Method

Grate the potato and onion into a clean tea towel. Squeeze out the water.

Add the egg, salt and pepper to the mix.

Heat a pan to hot. Add oil, and spoon of mix. Flatten to 1 cm thick. Fry for 3 min, then flip and fry some more.
