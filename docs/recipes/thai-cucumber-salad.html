Serves: 4
Cook Time: 10 min

Thai Cucumber Salad

From Green lite bites. I use this as a side with Thai Green Currey. There is no salt added in this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp lime juice
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • Â½ tsp tabasco
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • small handful mint, chopped
  • 25g cashew nuts, chopped

Method

Whisk together the lime juice, soy sauce, honey, tabasco and garlic to make the dressing.

Mix the cucumber, mint and cashew nuts together in a bowl, then mix in the dressing.
