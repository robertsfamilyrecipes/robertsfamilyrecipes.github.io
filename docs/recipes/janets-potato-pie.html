Janet's Potato Pie

We have this potato pie on new years eve. Its a long cook, up to 4 hours.

Ingredients

500g
stewing steak, in 2 cm dice
1
onion, chopped
1.5 kg
potato, peeled and diced to 2 cm
1
carrot, peeled and diced
1
stock cube, made to 500 ml of stock

For the pastry

75g
butter
150g
flour, sieved
½ tsp
table salt

Method

Add onions, meat and stock in a dish with a lid. Cook in oven on 160°C for 1 hour.

Add potatoes and carrot. Salt to taste. Cook for a further 2 hours until potatoes and meat are soft.

Make pastry, and refrigerate for 20 min. Roll out and add to pie.

Cook for a further 15-20 min until pastry is cooked.

Serve with peas and brown sauce.
