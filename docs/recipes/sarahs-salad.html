Sarah's Salad

Serves: 4
Cook Time: 45 min

Ingredients

  • Sweet Potatoes
  • sweet potatoes, peeled and chipped
  • 1 tin tomatoes
  • curry powder
  • cumin
  • salt
  • olive oil
  • 1 tin chickpeas
  • Dressing
  • hummus
  • olive oil
  • lemon juice
  • dijon mustard
  • maple syrup
  • salt and pepper
  • Salad
  • tomatoes, quartered and salted
  • cucumber, battons
  • lettuce, torn into pieces

Method

Sweet Potatoes. Peel and chop the sweet potatoes. Mix with the other ingredients. Then cremate in the oven on 210°C fan for 30-40 min. After 20 min add the chickpeas and mix round.

Dressing. Whisk together the dressing ingredients. Taste for salt.

Assemble. Mix the dressing with the sweet potatoes, plate, and pile the salad on top, with a bit more dressing.
