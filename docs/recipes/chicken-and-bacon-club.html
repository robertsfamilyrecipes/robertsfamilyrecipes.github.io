Serves: 4
Cook Time: 1 hour

Chicken and Bacon Club

This is an attempt to recreate the chicken and bacon club sandwich from Costa Coffee circa 2010. They no longer make that sandwich. The recipe is really too much to make on it's own - you kinda need to have the pasta sauce left over from a previous day.

Ingredients

  • Red Onions
  • 2 red onion sliced
  • salt
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp red vinegar
  • Pasta Sauce
  • see Bacon and Tomato Pasta
  • Chicken and Bacon
  • 250g bacon
  • 2 chicken breast, cut thin
  • 1 lemon
  • salt and pepper
  • mayonnaise
  • Sandwich
  • gruyère cheese, grated
  • 12 bread slices, lightly toasted

Method

Make the onions. Add onions, salt, sugar and vinegar to a small pan, and caramelize on medium to low heat. This takes 30+ min.

Pasta sauce. If the pasta sauce is already made, then warm in a small pan. You want to drive out most of the liquid, so it is drier than a normal pasta sauce. If it's not already made then follow the recipe here.

Make the chicken and bacon. Fry the chicken on high heat, add salt, and lemon juice. Set aside and allow to cool. Fry bacon on medium heat, and allow fat to render. Chop up chicken and bacon and mix with mayo.

Assemble the sandwich. Toast / pasta sauce and gruyère cheese / toast / chicken, bacon, mayo and red onions / toast.
