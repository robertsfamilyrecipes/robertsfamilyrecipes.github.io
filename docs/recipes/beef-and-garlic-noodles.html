Beef and Garlic Noodles

Serves: 4
Cook Time: 30 min

From Reddit

Ingredients

  • oil
  • 8 garlic cloves
  • 2 bunch spring onions
  • 750g beef (steak sizzlers/frying steak)
  • salt
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 4 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 pack noodles

Method

Cook the noodles following the packet directions. Drain.

Heat a wok to hot, add the beef, sugar, salt and fry. Reserve.

Turn down heat to medium, and fry garlic and spring onions for a couple of minutes. Reserve with the meat.

Turn heat to high, add noodles and soy sauce. Mix. Then add back in the reserved meat etc. and mix.
