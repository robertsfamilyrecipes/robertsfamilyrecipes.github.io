Serves: 4 as a side
Cook Time: 10 min

Quick Salsa

From Bite-sized Mexican

Ingredients

½
red onion, finley chopped
250g
tomatoes, chopped
1
green chilli, seeded and chopped
 
salt and pepper
1
lime, juiced

Method

Mix the onion, tomatoes and chilli. Salt and pepper.

Squeeze over the lime juice and mix.

Taste for salt.
