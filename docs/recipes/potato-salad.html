Potato Salad

Serves: 4
Cook Time: 20 min

Ingredients

  • 750g salad potato
  • 1 tbsp table salt
  • Dressing
  • handful basil
  • handful parsley
  • 2 lemons, juice
  • 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 4 garlic cloves, peeled
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp tahini
  • 1 tsp maple syrup
  • salt and pepper

Method

Potatoes. Boil potatoes till soft, about 20 min. I use 1.5 litre water and 1 tbsp table salt.

Dressing. Liquidate the dressing ingredients together. Taste for salt.

Assemble. Drain the potatoes and rinse with cold water to stop the cooking. Mix in the dressing.
