Serves: 4
Cook Time: 1 hour

Columbian beans

Adapted from Columbian Bean by Erica Dinho. The original recipe is a long cook and uses plantain and pork hock - which are not easy to get hold of. This is an anglicised version of the recipe.

Ingredients

  • 250g pack bacon, diced
  • ½ onion, finley chopped
  • bunch spring onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • ¼ tsp ground cumin
  • sunflower oil
  • 2 400g tin borlotti beans, drained
  • 1 carrot, cut into battons
  • ½ potato, diced small
  • ½ banana, mashed
  • ½ 400g tin tomatoes, chopped
  • chicken stock pot
  • 250ml water
  • salt and pepper

Method

Cook the bacon. Heat a pan to medium and fry the bacon so the fat renders and it browns a bit.

Turn the heat to low, and fry the onions till translucent. Add the garlic, spring onions and cumin and cook for 2 min.

Add the beans, carrot, potato, banana, tomatoes, water and stock cube. Bring to the boil, then simmer for 30 min. The potatoes should thicken the 'soup'.

Taste for salt.
