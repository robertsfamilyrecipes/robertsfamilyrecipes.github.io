Salmon Pasta Salad

Ingredients

300g
tomatoes, chopped and salted
4-6
salmon steaks
1 bag (~150g)
rocket
500g
pasta

For the dressing

4 tbsp
olive oil
1 tbsp
white wine vinegar
Â¼ tsp
dijon mustard

Method

Heat the pan till decently hot. Drizzle with olive oil and add the salmon steaks, skin side down. Salt and pepper the salmon. Cook for 5 min or so, then flip and cook the other side for another 5 min. While this is going on, scrape of the salmon skin.

Whisk together the olive oil, white wine vinegar and dijon mustard in a small bowl, to make the salad dressing.

Cook the pasta following packet timings. I use 1 tbsp table salt and 1.5 liter of water.

Drain the pasta, and add back to the pan. Mix in the dressing, then stir in the tomatoes and rocket.

Serve onto plates, and add the salmon on top.
