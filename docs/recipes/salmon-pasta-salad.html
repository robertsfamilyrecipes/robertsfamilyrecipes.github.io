Salmon Pasta Salad

Ingredients

300g
tomatoes, chopped and salted
4-6
salmon steaks
1 bag (~150g)
rocket
500g
pasta

For the dressing

4 tbsp
olive oil
1 tbsp
white wine vinegar
1/4 tsp
dijon mustard

Method

Heat the pad till decently hot, drizzle with olive oil, and the salmon steaks - skin side down.
Salt and pepper the salmon.
Cook for 5 min or so, then flip and cook the other side for another 5 min.
While this is going on, scrape of the salmon skin.

Whisk together the olive oil, white wine vinegar and dijon mustard in a small bowl, to make the salad dressing.

Cook the pasta following packet timings, with 1 tbsp table salt in the pasta water.
Drain the pasta.

Back in the pan, mix together the pasta, tomatoes and rocket, then stir in the dressing.

Serve onto plates, and add the salmon on top.
