Ham and Cheese Croissant

Serves: 2
Cook Time: 30 min

From reddit. Serve with chips.

Ingredients

  • 4 croissant
  • 250g ham
  • 250g brie, sliced
  • dijon mustard
  • cocktail gherkins - cut in half

Method

Heat the oven to 180°C fan.

Prepare the croissant. Cut in half, and spread dijon mustard on the bottom half. Pile on ham, then gherkins then cheese.

Bake in oven for 7 minutes.
