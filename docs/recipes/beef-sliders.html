Beef Sliders

Serves: 4
Cook Time: 1 hour

By morganeisenberg on reddit and hostthetoast.

Ingredients

  • 100g butter
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 500g beef mince
  • 1 beef stock pot
  • 2 tsp worcestershire sauce
  • salt and pepper
  • 12 slider buns
  • 200g gruyere cheese, grated
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds
  • 1 tsp fresh thyme
  • ½ tsp onion powder
  • ½ tsp garlic powder

Method

Onions. Melt 25g butter, and caramelize the onions. Takes about 30 min.

Beef. Mix beef, ½ stockpot, 1 tsp worcestershire sauce, salt and pepper. Add to the onions and brown the beef, mixing as you go.

Sauce. Melt 75g butter, add ½ stockpot, 1 tsp worcestershire sauce, and mix.

Buns. Put the bun bottoms in a tray, add beef, cheese, and top bun.

Then brush over sauce, sprinkle with sesame seeds, thyme, onion powder and garlic powder.

Cook in oven on 160°C fan for 20 min, until the cheese is melted.
