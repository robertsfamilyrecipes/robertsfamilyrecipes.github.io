### Ingredients

* 1 onion, sliced into rings
* 300ml buttermilk
* 2 egg whites
* 4 tbsp flour
* 4 tbsp cornmeal
* 4 tbsp panko breadcrumbs
* 1 tsp garlic powder
* 1 tsp smoked paprika
* salt and pepper.
* butter

### Method

Onions.  Mix onion rings with the buttermilk.  Ideally refrigerate for 4 hours, but who's got time for that.

Egg whites.  Whisk up the egg whites in a bowl.

Mix.  Mix the flour, cornmeal, breadcrumbs, garlic powder, paprika, salt and pepper in a bowl.

Prepare.  Working in sequence, dip the onion ring in the egg whites, the the flour mix, then place on a tray.  Dot with butter.

Cook in oven on 200&deg;C fan for 30 min.
