From [reddit](https://www.reddit.com/r/GifRecipes/comments/ihiump/taleggio_ham_cornichon_baked_croissants/).  Serve with chips.

### Ingredients

* 4 croissant
* 250g ham
* 250g brie, sliced
* dijon mustard
* cocktail gherkins - cut in half

### Method

Heat the oven to 180&deg;C fan.

Prepare the croissant.  Cut in half, and spread dijon mustard on the bottom half.  Pile on ham, then gherkins then cheese.

Bake in oven for 7 minutes.
