By morganeisenberg on [reddit](https://www.reddit.com/r/GifRecipes/comments/emqde1/french_onion_beef_sliders/) and [hostthetoast](https://hostthetoast.com/french-onion-beef-sliders/).  Sliders are small burger buns.


### Ingredients

* 100g butter
* 1 onion, sliced
* 500g beef mince
* 1 beef stock pot
* 2 tsp worcestershire sauce
* salt and pepper
* 12 slider buns
* 200g gruyere cheese, grated
* 1 tbsp sesame seeds
* 1 tsp fresh thyme
* &frac12; tsp onion powder
* &frac12; tsp garlic powder

### Method

Onions.  Melt 25g butter, and caramelize the onions.  Takes about 30 min.

Beef.  Mix beef, &frac12; stockpot, 1 tsp worcestershire sauce, pepper.  Add to the onions and brown the beef, mixing as you go.  Taste for salt.

Sauce.  Melt 75g butter, add &frac12; stockpot, 1 tsp worcestershire sauce, and mix.

Buns.  Put the bun bottoms in a tray, add beef, cheese, and top bun.  

Then brush over sauce, sprinkle with sesame seeds, thyme, onion powder and garlic powder.

Cook in oven on 160&deg;C fan for 15 min, until the cheese is melted.
