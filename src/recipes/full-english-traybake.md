### Ingredients

* handful parsley, finley chopped
* 3 garlic cloves, mashed
* 250g chestnut mushrooms, wiped, stalked and quartered
* olive oil
* salt
* &frac12; sourdough loaf, pulled into bits
* 300g pack bacon, halved
* 375g pack sausages, halved
* 250g cherry tomatoes, halved and lightly salted
* 4 eggs
* salt

### Method

Heat the oven to 180&deg;C.

Mushrooms.  Add parsley, garlic and oil to a bowl and mix.  Add the mushrooms, salt and mix.

Tray.  Lay over the bits of bread, bacon, sausages, mushrooms and tomatoes over the bread.

Cover with foil and bake for 20 mins.

Bake for another 10 mins without the foil.

Crack the eggs on top and bake for 6 mins just until the whites are set.

Serve.  With beans.
