### Ingredients

* 7 limes, juiced
* 25ml tequila
* 2 tbsp golden syrup
* 80g caster sugar

### Method

Juice the limes, then top up with water to 300ml.  Mix in tequila, golden syrup and sugar.  Dissolve.

Chill in a ice cream machine until set.
