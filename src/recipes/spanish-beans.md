### Ingredients

250g
:   tin broad beans

3 tbsp
:   olive oil

1 
:   onion, finely chopped

5
:   garlic cloves

1 sprig 
:   fresh oregano

90g 
:   chorizo

1
:   lemon, zest and juice

&nbsp;
:   Sea salt and freshly ground black pepper

### Method

Cook onions.  Add garlic, oregano, chorizo cook some more

Add broad beans and cook 2 min

Finish with lemon
