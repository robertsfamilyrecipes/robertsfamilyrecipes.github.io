From [Green lite bites](https://greenlitebites.com/thai-inspired-cucumber-cashew-salad/).  I use this as a side with Thai Green Currey.  There is no salt added in this recipe.

### Ingredients

2 tbsp
:   lime juice

1 tsp
:   soy sauce

1 tbsp
:   honey

&frac12; tsp
:   tabasco

1
:   garlic clove, minced

1 
:   cucumber, diced

handful
:   mint, chopped

25g
:   cashew nuts, chopped

### Method

Whisk together the lime juice, soy sauce, honey, tabasco and garlic to make the dressing.

Mix the cucumber, mint and cashew nuts together in a bowl, then mix in the dressing.
