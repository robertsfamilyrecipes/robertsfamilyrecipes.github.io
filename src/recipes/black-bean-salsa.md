From [reddit](https://old.reddit.com/r/SalsaSnobs/comments/l9n74v/made_some_black_bean_salsa_i_have_severe_pain/).  Cilantro tastes like soap to me, so I don't put that in.

### Ingredients

* 1 400g tin black beans, drained and rinsed
* 1 200g tin sweetcorn, drained
* 1 red onion, chopped fine
* 1 green pepper, diced
* 1 red pepper, diced
* 1 jalapeños, chopped fine
* 4 limes, juiced
* cilantro, chopped
* salt and pepper      

### Method

Chop the ingredients that need chopping, then mix together in a bowl with the lime juice. Taste for salt.
