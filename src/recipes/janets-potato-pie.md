### Ingredients

500g
:   Stewing Steak, in 2 cm dice

1
:   Onion, chopped

1.5 kg
:   Potato, peeled and diced to 2 cm

1
:   Carrot, peeled and diced

1
:   Stock cube, made to 500 ml of stock

For the pastry

75g
:   Butter

150g
:   Flour, sieved

&frac12; tsp
:   table salt



### Method

Add onions, meat and stock in a dish with a lid
Put in oven on 160C, and cook for 1 hour

Add potatoes and carrot
Add salt to taste, and cook for 2 hour
until potatoes and meat are soft

Make pastry, and refrigerate for 20 min
Roll out and add to pie

Cook for a further 15-20 min until pastry is cooked

Serve with peas and brown sauce

Total cooking time ~ 3 1/2 hours
