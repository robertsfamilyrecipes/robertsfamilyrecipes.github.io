[From bbc good food](https://www.bbcgoodfood.com/recipes/scampi-po-boys).  Serve with chips and salad from the spare lettuce.

### Ingredients

* 2 part baked baguettes
* 200g frozen scampi
* &frac12; iceberg lettuce, torn into pieces
* 2 tomatoes, sliced and salted
* 4 gherkins, chopped
* 1 garlic clove, minced
* 3 tbsp mayonnaise
* 1 tbsp hot sauce

### Method

Bake the scampi in the oven, following packet directions.

Bake the bread in the oven, following packet instructions.

Make the dressing, by whisking together the garlic, mayonnaise and hot sauce.

Assemble.  Cut the bread in half and spread over the dressing.  Slap on the scampi, gherkins, lettuce and tomatoes.
