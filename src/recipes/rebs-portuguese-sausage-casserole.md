### Notes
Family recipe from Reb Willis.  
**Amount need refinement...this makes a lot**

### Ingredients

3 tbsp     
:   olive oil

3          
:   onions

400g
:   chorizo, diced

3          
:   potatoes, peeled and chopped to 3cm cube

6 tin      
:   beans (chick pea, kidney, borlotti, white)

1
:   green pepper, chopped

2 tin      
:   tomatoes, chopped

2 cloves
:   garlic

&frac12; tsp    
:   dried basil

&frac12; tsp    
:   dried thyme

2          
:   bay leaves

1 
:   chicken stock pot

60g
:   panko breadcrumbs

### Method

Saut√© onion until golden.

Add chorizo cook another 10 min.

Add garlic, thyme, basil, bay and cook for another minute.

Add tomatoes.

Drains and rinse beans.  Add beans, potatoes, stock.

Cook low heat 20 min.

Add green pepper another 15 min.

Top with breadcrumbs.

Cook in oven until brown, 1 hour, 180C fan.

Serve with tabasco.
