### Ingredients

&frac12;
:   white cabbage, shredded

1
:   carrot, peeled and grated

2 tsp
:   vinegar

1 tbsp
:   sugar

1 tsp
:   dijon mustard

&frac12; tsp
:   salt

4 tbsp
:   mayonnaise

### Method

Whisk together vinegar, sugar, salt and mustard until sugar and salt dissolved.

Add mayonnaise and whisk.

Combine with cabbage and carrots.

Refrigerate for 2 hours.
