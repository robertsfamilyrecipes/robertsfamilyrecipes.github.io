### Ingredients

* 1 pack (375g) sausage, cut into pieces
* 450g cherry tomatoes
* 400g   tin chickpeas, drained
* 2 large carrots, peeled and diced
* olive oil
* 1 tsp  cumin seeds
* 1 tsp  aleppo chilli flakes
* salt and pepper
* &frac12; tsp  smoked paprika
* _Half way through_
* 300g spinach
* _At the end_
* 30g toasted salted pumpkin seeds
* 1 lemon, zest and juice
* 2 tbsp chopped dill

### Method

Heat the oven to 180&deg;C fan. Put the sausages, tomatoes, chickpeas, carrots in a tray.  Mix in olive oil, cumin seeds, chilli flakes, salt, pepper, paprika and toss to coat.

Add 120ml water and roast for 45 minutes, until the sausages start to brown. Rotate the pan once halfway through the cooking, and add the spinach.

Sprinkle the pumpkin seeds on top, add the lemon juice and zest, and the dill, and serve.
