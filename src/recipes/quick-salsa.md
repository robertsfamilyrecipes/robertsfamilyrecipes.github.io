From [Bite-sized Mexican](https://www.amazon.co.uk/Bite-sized-Mexican-Anon/dp/0600603261/)

### Ingredients

* &frac12; red onion, finley chopped
* 250g tomatoes, chopped and salted
* 1 green chilli, seeded and chopped
* pepper
* 1 lime, juiced

### Method

Mix the onion, tomatoes and chilli.  Salt and pepper.

Squeeze over the lime juice and mix.

Taste for salt.
